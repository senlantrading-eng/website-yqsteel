Medium Plate

Hot-rolled steel plate 8-250mm for load-bearing structures and fabrication

Product Description

Medium steel plates are hot-rolled flat steel products with thickness ranging from 8mm to 250mm. These plates provide excellent strength and durability for heavy-duty structural applications, machinery manufacturing, and industrial fabrication.

Key Characteristics:

  • • High strength for load-bearing applications
  • • Excellent weldability and formability
  • • Wide thickness range for various needs
  • • Suitable for cutting, drilling, and machining
