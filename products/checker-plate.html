Checker Plate

Diamond pattern anti-slip steel plate for safety-critical surfaces

Product Description

Checker plates have a raised pattern surface for anti-slip performance. Widely used for walkways, stairs, platforms, vehicle floors, and industrial flooring.

Key Characteristics:

  • • Raised diamond pattern for anti-slip performance
  • • Enhanced safety for walkways and stairs
  • • Durable and wear-resistant surface
  • • Suitable for industrial and commercial use
