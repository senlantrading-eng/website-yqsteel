H Beam

Hot-rolled H-shaped structural steel for high-load applications

Product Description

H beams (also known as H-sections or wide flange beams) feature an H-shaped cross-section with parallel inner flange surfaces. This design provides excellent load-bearing capacity and structural stability with optimal strength-to-weight ratio.

Key Characteristics:

  • • High bending resistance for vertical and horizontal loads
  • • Parallel flange surfaces for easy connection
  • • Efficient material distribution reduces weight
  • • Standardized dimensions for design consistency
