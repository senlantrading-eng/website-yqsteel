I Beam

Universal beam for general construction applications

Product Description

I beams feature an I-shaped cross-section with tapered flanges. They offer good bending resistance and are cost-effective for many construction applications requiring moderate load-bearing capacity.

Key Characteristics:

  • • Good bending resistance for vertical loads
  • • Cost-effective for general construction
  • • Wide availability in standard sizes
  • • Easy to fabricate and connect
