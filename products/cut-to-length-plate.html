Cut-to-Length Plate

Leveled steel sheets 1.5-19.75mm with excellent flatness for fabrication

Product Description

Cut-to-length plates are produced by leveling and shearing hot-rolled steel coils into flat sheets. This process provides excellent flatness and dimensional accuracy, making these plates ideal for precision fabrication, laser cutting, and general manufacturing applications.

Key Characteristics:

  • • Superior flatness compared to standard plates
  • • Precise dimensional control
  • • Clean edges from shearing process
  • • Ready for immediate fabrication
