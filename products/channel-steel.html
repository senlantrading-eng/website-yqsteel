Channel Steel

U-shaped structural steel for versatile fabrication and installation

Product Description

Channel steel is widely used for purlins, brackets, frames, and equipment bases. It is easy to fabricate and install, suitable for a wide range of structural uses.

Key Characteristics:

  • • U-shaped cross-section for versatile applications
  • • Easy to fabricate and install
  • • Excellent for purlins and support structures
  • • Suitable for equipment bases and frames
