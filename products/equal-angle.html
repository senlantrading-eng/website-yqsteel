Equal Angle Steel

L-shaped structural steel with equal legs for versatile connections

Equal angles are versatile for brackets, frames, connection parts, and reinforcement in steel structures. Suitable for welding and drilling.

  • • Excellent for brackets and connection parts

  • • Ideal for reinforcement in steel structures
