About YQ Steel

Tangshan: China's Steel Capital

The world's largest steelmaking hub and our strategic home base

📍 Our Location Advantage

Located in Tangshan, Hebei Province — the world's largest steelmaking hub. This city alone produces over 130 million tons of steel annually, accounting for approximately 14% of China's total steel production and 8% of global output.

Tangshan's industrial legacy dates back to 1878 with the establishment of the Kailuan Coal Mine. Today, it remains the birthplace of China's modern steel industry, home to major producers like HBIS Group and over 30 major steel enterprises.
